## Tone of Voice

- **Professional** — not casual, not overly corporate
- **Reliable** — factual, confident, no exaggeration
- **Industrial** — use technical product names correctly
- **Customer-first** — focus on buyer benefits, not just company pride

### Example Headlines
✅ `Trusted Manufacturer of Direct Dyes Since 1990`
✅ `Consistent Quality. Reliable Supply. 35+ Years.`
❌ `Best Dye Company in the World`
❌ `Amazing Colors for Your Products`

---

## Logo Usage

- Always use the full wordmark: **Sanjay Industries**
- Subtitle tag: `DYES & CHEMICALS` in uppercase, light weight
- Minimum size: 120px wide
- Clear space: equal to the height of the "S" on all sides
- **Do not** stretch, rotate, or apply color filters

---

## What This Website Is NOT

- ❌ Not an e-commerce store
- ❌ Not a startup or SaaS product
- ❌ Not colorful or playful
- ❌ Not flashy animations or heavy gradients

## What This Website IS

- ✅ Premium B2B industrial corporate website
- ✅ Trust-building and inquiry generation
- ✅ Clean, restrained, professional
- ✅ Inspired by: BASF, Siemens, Honeywell, Tata Chemicals
